PolyU–Huawei Mathematical Optimization Innovation Laboratory (MOILab) advances intelligent optimization by integrating optimization algorithms with Huawei XPU acceleration to build scalable, high-performance solver technologies and intelligent optimization platforms. The laboratory focuses on five major research directions, including cluster-based solver technology, embedded solver technology, LLM inference acceleration, optimization in mobile devices, and power grid scheduling. Its vision is to combine the strengths of optimization algorithms and Huawei XPU acceleration to form an intelligent optimization platform that empowers multiple key domains such as artificial intelligence, energy and smart grid, telecommunications and networks, and intelligent production. Through this collaboration, MOILab is committed to developing XPU-accelerated advanced solvers for large-scale mathematical optimization, bridging theory and practice through algorithmic innovation, and promoting knowledge transfer and real-world deployment in collaboration with industry partners. The laboratory also strives to nurture the next generation of researchers and engineers, driving forward the development of mathematical optimization and high-performance computing.

    PolyU × Huawei — Together, we optimize the future.

    Objectives of MOILab:

    • Develop XPU-accelerated advanced optimization solvers for large-scale problems.
    • Bridge theory and practice with cutting-edge algorithm design and open, reproducible systems.
    • Enable real-world deployment via industry–academia collaboration and technology transfer.
    • Train next-generation researchers and engineers in optimization and high-performance computing.
